Card: Disciplined self-indulgence

For this project, I interpreted "Disciplined self-indulgence" as the balance between personal desire and structural constraints. While clicking to place my favorite stickers represents "self-indulgence," I implemented a strict grid and time limits to represent "discipline." This suggests that even in our passions, there must be an underlying order and restraint to maintain balance.
