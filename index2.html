Card: Disciplined self-indulgence

I chose the card "Disciplined self-indulgence" and interpreted it through a relatable student perspective. The project features a dog that increasingly indulges in alcohol (soju) as the user clicks, representing self-indulgence. However, to show discipline, I implemented "reality" buttons like assignments and classes that reset the state. It visualizes the constant effort to enjoy what we love while maintaining the responsibility to wake up for our daily duties.
